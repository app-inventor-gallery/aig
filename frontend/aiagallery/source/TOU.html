Description of MIT App Inventor Gallery Site

You can access MIT App Inventor software, which lets you develop applications for Android devices using a web browser and either a connected phone or emulator, at the MIT App Inventor website. This Site allows you to upload your own projects and to access projects uploaded by others. As part of this community, you are sharing projects with and commenting on projects developed by people from many different cultures and countries, of many different ages, with all levels of experience. Community Guidelines

Do’s

We need your help to make this community a supportive place for every member. Here is how you can help:
  • Be respectful. When sharing projects or posting comments, remember that people of many different ages and backgrounds will see your contributions.
  • Offer constructive comments. Rather than criticize a project or forum post, say what you like about it and offer suggestions on how to make it better.
  • Give credit. Feel free to make modified versions of other people’s projects – just make sure to give them credit.
  • Help keep the site friendly. If you feel that others would find a project or comment mean, insulting or otherwise inappropriate, click the link that says “flag as inappropriate.” The MIT App Inventor Gallery team will review, and may remove any project, comment, or post.
