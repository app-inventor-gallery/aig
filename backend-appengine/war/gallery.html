App Inventor Gallery

The App Inventor Gallery is an on-line studio allowing App Inventor developers to collaborate on building apps and learning programming. Think of it as an open-source (blocks) Google Play app market and an on-line learning studio for beginning app developers.

Gallery now has...

  • 13,700+ recorded users
  • 880+ apps uploaded
  • 84,300+ apps viewed
  • 41,900+ apps downloaded

And it's still rapidly growing in its beta state.

The Gallery is being developed as part of a collaborative project including USF, UMass-Lowell and MIT. USF's Vincent Zhang and David Wolber are leading the development of the current version, along with Fred Martin and James DeFilippo of UMass-Lowell. The system was originally architected and developed by Derrell Lipman and Fred Martin of UMass-Lowell.
